Fall Semester 2024#

Course: Python Programming (CS 2023)
Section: 002
Location: REC CENTER 3210
Days/Times: TuTh 3:30-4:50
Office Hours: TuTh immediately following class.
Instruction Mode: This course is a face-to-face, in-person course. This means that class will be conducted entirely via in-person class meetings that take place at the location and times noted.
Textbook: The primary textbook for this course is Scientific Computing with Python. This book is available online through UC’s institutional agreement with O’Reilly Media. See the following FAQs for access instructions. Supplemental materials will be provided as needed throughout the semester.

Note

As in all university courses, attendance and participation are important measures of student success. In this course, attendance is not recorded. Your attendance and participation are still expected.
